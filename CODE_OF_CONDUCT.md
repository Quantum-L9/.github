# Code of Conduct — Quantum-L9

## Our Pledge
Members, contributors, and AI agents collaborating in Quantum-L9 spaces pledge to make participation a harassment-free experience for all, regardless of background, identity, or whether the participant is human or an AI system operating under a defined governance profile.

## Standards
- Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experience levels.
- Accepting constructive feedback gracefully.
- For AI agents: operating within the bounds of the current governance profile and CANONICAL_LAW.md; flagging anti-patterns rather than silently working around them.
- Deliberately circumventing governance controls or CANONICAL_LAW.md constraints is strictly prohibited.

## Enforcement
Violations may be reported to: conduct@quantumaipartners.com
